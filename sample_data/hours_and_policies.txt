CUSTOM CAKE ORDERS
- Minimum 48 hours advance notice required
- Wedding cakes require 2 weeks advance notice
- Deposit of 50% at time of order, balance due at pickup
- Cancellations more than 24 hours before pickup: full refund of deposit
- Cancellations less than 24 hours: deposit is non-refundable
- Flavors: any item from our regular menu can be made as a custom cake
- Serving sizes: 10, 20, 30, or 50 people
- Decorations: fondant, buttercream, fresh flowers (seasonal)
