Sweet Rise Bakery — Allergen Information

ALLERGEN POLICY
All items are prepared in a kitchen that handles wheat, dairy, eggs, tree nuts,
and soy. While we take precautions to avoid cross-contamination, we cannot
guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.

NUT-FREE OPTIONS
The following items are prepared in a dedicated nut-free prep area:
- Vanilla Cloud Cake
- Lemon Drizzle Cake
- All cupcakes except Almond Croissant
- Sourdough Loaf
- Whole Wheat Bread

VEGAN OPTIONS
Made without any animal products:
- Chocolate Avocado Cake
- Coconut Berry Tart
- Gluten-Free Chocolate Cupcake
- Olive Focaccia
- Baguette
- All drinks can be made with oat milk at no extra charge

GLUTEN-FREE OPTIONS
- Gluten-Free Chocolate Cupcake
- Coconut Berry Tart (naturally gluten-free)
Note: Other items contain wheat flour.

DAIRY-FREE OPTIONS
All vegan items are also dairy-free. Additionally:
- Sourdough Loaf (no dairy)
- Fresh Orange Juice

For specific allergen questions, please ask our staff. We keep a detailed
ingredient list for every item.
